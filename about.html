About Us

"Do what you love and you won't have to work a single day for the rest of your life". We, at The Golden String live and breathe by this rule. Music is not just a business for us, it's our passion. In 1989, our founder Tim Morgan started humbly when he opened up a small guitar repair and buying/selling shop in Calgary. Fast forward 26 years (yes, we just celebrated our silver jubilee), we are now one of the major music businesses in Canada.

Over the years we have proudly incorporated a number of music related areas and dimensions in our business and this is just a start. We look forward to establishing our own recording studios and ultimately founding a record label one day that will help struggling artists realize their dreams and serve the greater art that is music. For now, we feel proud of the fact that because of The Golden String, countless Canadians play and enjoy music in one form or another and tonnes of our nation's kids have learned to play music because we were and are here for them.
